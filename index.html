Latest News & Updates

New Engine Regulations Announced for 2026

The FIA has confirmed major changes to engine regulations, focusing on sustainable fuels and hybrid power units for the 2026 season. Teams are already working on new designs to adapt to the rules.

Verstappen extends contract with Red Bull

Reigning world champion Max Verstappen has signed a long-term contract extension with Red Bull Racing, solidifying his future with the team until 2028.

Pirelli tests new tire compounds for upcoming season

Pirelli conducted a successful test session in Barcelona, trialing new tire compounds designed to improve durability and reduce overheating during races. The data gathered will be crucial for the next season.
