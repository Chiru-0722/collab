About Formula 1

Formula 1, often abbreviated to F1, represents the pinnacle of international motorsport. As a world championship governed by the FIA, it combines cutting-edge technology, strategic racing, and elite athleticism. Teams compete for the Constructors' Championship, while drivers race for the Drivers' World Championship, with each Grand Prix serving as a high-stakes chapter in the season's unfolding narrative.
