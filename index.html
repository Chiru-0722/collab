About Formula 1

Formula 1, or F1, is the pinnacle of motorsport, known for its high-speed racing, advanced technology, and global appeal. It is a single-seater car racing competition governed by the Fédération Internationale de l'Automobile (FIA). The F1 season consists of a series of races, known as Grand Prix, held on circuits worldwide. Teams compete for the Constructors' Championship, while drivers vie for the Drivers' Championship.
